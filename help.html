Help & Documentation

Operational guidance for PrivGuard modules (SOC-style).
How Password Analyzer Works
Entropy scoring, pattern detection, and weakness indicators are used to estimate resilience against offline guessing.
How Phishing URL Checker Works
Structural indicators + upstream threat intel are aggregated into a weighted risk model and classification.
How Email Header Analyzer Works
Parses Received chain, extracts origin IP, checks alignment signals, and produces a forensic recommendation.
How IP Reputation Tool Works
Aggregates multi-source intel (abuse confidence, detections, ASN/ISP context) into a normalized 0–100 score.
How File Scanner Works
Scans file characteristics (extension, entropy, signatures/indicators) and produces a risk classification.
Data Privacy Notice
PrivGuard is designed to minimize exposure of sensitive content. Avoid submitting secrets to third-party services unless policy allows.
